1. Theory: What is EDA?
EDA is the process of exploring data before modeling.
It helps you:

>Understand the shape of distributions (normal, skewed, bimodal).
>Spot anomalies or outliers.
>Compare groups (e.g., male vs female spending).
>Identify correlations between variables.

2. Key Pandas & Seaborn Functions

df.describe() → summary statistics

df.corr() → correlation matrix

sns.histplot() → distribution plots

sns.barplot() → category comparisons

sns.scatterplot() → relationships between variables

sns.heatmap() → visualize correlations
